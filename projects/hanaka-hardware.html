Hanaka Hardware

A comprehensive ecommerce platform born from a desire to support local hardware stores through real time inventory management and seamless payment integration.

Real-time DB WordPress CMS

The Vision

Hanaka Hardware is more than an ecommerce platform; it's a vision for the renaissance of local trade. I recognized that the heart of our community beats in its hardware stores, yet many were held back by fragmented systems. I built Hanaka to unify these voices, providing a digital foundation that allows local businesses to compete with absolute clarity and modern efficiency.

A Source of Truth

At its core, Hanaka leverages real-time synchronization to solve the age-old problem of stock discrepancies. By architecting a system where every transaction ripples instantly across all branch databases, I've created a single, breathing source of truth. This technical precision builds the most valuable asset in any business: unwavering customer trust.

The Challenge of Synchronization

Maintaining accurate stock levels across multiple branch locations while users are making simultaneous purchases was a significant technical hurdle. I recognized that even a single discrepancy could erode customer trust and disrupt the heart of the business.

To ensure a single, breathing source of truth, I implemented Supabase Postgres triggers and real-time subscriptions. Every time a checkout begins, the system protects the specific inventory record and uses a broadcast channel to update all connected clients instantly, ensuring that both merchants and customers see the exact truth at all times.

Key Features

Smart Inventory

Dynamic stock tracking with automatic alerts for branch managers when items are running low.

Payment Integration

Full Paynow support for EcoCash, OneMoney, and Visa or Mastercard payments.

Multi Branch Support

The ability for users to select their nearest branch and check availability locally.

Admin Dashboard

Real time sales analytics and inventory management built for the heart of the business.

The WordPress Evolution

Why WordPress?

As Hanaka Hardware prepares to scale, I am integrating **Headless WordPress** to serve as the powerful Content Management System (CMS). This transition is vital for several reasons:

  • Content Empowerment: Allowing store managers who are not technical to easily update product descriptions and promotion banners without needing to touch a single line of code.
  • SEO Dominance: Leveraging the mature SEO ecosystem of WordPress to ensure Hanaka Hardware ranks at the top of local search results.
  • Scalable Marketing: Utilizing built in marketing tools and plugins to run targeted campaigns and reward our regular customers.

This project is currently in active development as part of my new tech venture.
