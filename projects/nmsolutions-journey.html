The Inbox Journey

A technical deep dive into how I revolutionized email deliverability for NMSolutions, moving vital communications from spam to the primary inbox.

The Challenge of Presence

When NMSolutions first started sending automated project updates and client invoices, we noticed a critical issue: over 70% of our emails were landing in the "Spam" or "Junk" folders. This led to delayed payments and missed communications.

Technical Stack Used

The Implementation

1. Audit & Authentication

I started by auditing our DNS records. I implemented SPF records to authorize our sending servers and configured 2048 bit DKIM keys to cryptographically sign every outgoing email.

2. DMARC Enforcement

I set up a DMARC policy initially set to "none" to monitor traffic, later moving it to "quarantine" as we reached 100% alignment across all sending sources.

3. Reputation Management

I cleaned our mailing lists to reduce bounce rates and implemented a warm up schedule for our new dedicated IP, gradually increasing volume to build trust with major ISPs like Google and Microsoft.

The Result

Within 30 days, our deliverability rate soared. Tests showed that our emails were now reaching the primary inbox for 98% of recipients. This transition didn't just fix a technical bug; it restored trust in our digital infrastructure.
