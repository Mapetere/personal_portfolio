Plant Triage

A gentle AI powered companion for home gardeners, designed to diagnose plant issues through a calm and observational lens.

React OpenAI/Gemini API Tailwind CSS Vite

The Harmony of Code and Nature

Plant Triage was born from a desire to reconcile technical precision with the organic growth of the natural world. I wanted to move away from data-heavy, clinical interfaces to create a space that feels like a quiet walk through a garden—light, airy, and focused entirely on the health of the plants.

The Language of Care

Instead of definitive, cold diagnoses, Plant Triage offers "gentle observations" and "potential paths for care," acknowledging the nuance of plant health and the home gardener's perspective as a steward of their green space.

Key Features

AI Diagnosis

Leverages advanced vision models to analyze plant photos and identify pests, diseases, or nutrient deficiencies.

Calm UI/UX

A nature inspired color palette and soft typography create a stress free environment for worried plant parents.

Contextual Advice

Provides actionable steps based on the specific plant species and identified symptoms.

Mobile First Design

Optimized for quick use in the garden, allowing users to snap a photo and get results instantly.
